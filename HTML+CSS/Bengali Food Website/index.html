About us

Bengali food is often served on plates which have a distinct flowery pattern often in blue or pink. Another characteristic of Bengali food is the use the boti (also called dao or da). It is a long curved blade on a platform held down by one or both feet; both hands are used to hold whatever is being cut and move it against the blade, which faces the user.

Our food quality

Bengali foods are culturally lively and simply delicious, ranging from yummy curries to intricate sweets and confectioneries. Cham cham, Sandesh, Daab Chingri are just a few examples of the scrumptious and tantalizing delicacies of the beautiful Bengali cuisine.
